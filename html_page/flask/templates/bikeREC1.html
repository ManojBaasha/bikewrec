What is bikeREC?

bikeREC is an application where the user can upload an image of any bike that they wish to view online.
Using our image recognition technonogy, our application finds the most relevant bikes on all major
online stores and these links show up on our website for the user to view.



Our application saves the users hours of finding the model of the bike that they have in mind and
automatically finds platforms where the user can directly purchase the bike.
